Eyelash Extension Services

We specialize in eyelash extensions that enhance your natural beauty with precision and care. Our range of styles from natural to hybrid to full glamour, which are expertly tailored to complement your unique eye shape and personal style.

In addition, we offer advanced lash lifting treatments to give your natural lashes a lasting curl and lift, creating a refreshed and polished look.

Our skilled technicians use only the highest quality products, ensuring both the health and durability of your lashes. We prioritize a comfortable, safe, and personalized experience from your initial consultation through to the final application.

Natural Lash

Our Natural Lash extensions are perfect for those who want a subtle enhancement that highlights their eyes without overpowering their natural beauty. Ideal for everyday wear, this style suits clients seeking a soft, elegant look with gentle length and volume.

Glamour Lash

Our Glamour Lash extensions are designed for those who want to make a bold, striking impression. Perfect for special occasions or anyone who loves a dramatic, ful-volume look, these lashes add significant length and thickness for captivating eyes. Tailored to enhance your unique eye shape, this style delivers undeniable allure and elegance.

CHF.- 120

Hybrid Lash

Our Hybrid Lash extensions combine the best of both worlds, blending classic and volume techniques for a textured, multi-dimensional look. This versatile style is perfect for clients who want a fuller appearance without the extreme volume of our Glamour Lash. Ideal for those seeking a balanced, yet striking enhancement that suits any occasion.

CHF.- 170

Mascara Look Lash

Our Mascara Look Lash extensions are designed to mimic the effect of wearing mascara. This style is perfect for those who want the look of fuller, darker lashes without the daily application of mascara. Ideal for a polished, everyday look, these lashes offer a subtle boost to your natural beauty.

CHF.- 150

Lash Lifting

Our Lash Lifting service provides a semi-permanent curl to your natural lashes, giving them a lifted, wide-eyed appearance without the need for extensions. This treatment is perfect for those who want to enhance their natural lashes with a long-lasting curl that opens up the eyes and adds definition.

CHF.- 120
